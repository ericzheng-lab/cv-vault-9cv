What does Senior Producer do at WPP Production?

A Senior Producer at WPP Production is a dynamic individual who takes charge! Responsible for overseeing all facets of integrated production, including scoping, concept development, budget management, scheduling, talent negotiations and payment, as well as overseeing shoots, editing, visual effects, music selection, and final delivery. They excel in cultivating and utilizing vendor partnerships to assemble the ideal team for each project's needs. Additionally, the Senior Producer collaborates closely with the digital, social, and experiential teams to ensure seamless integration of assets and content across various channels.

Reporting lines and key stakeholders

This role reports to the Executive Producer/Chief Production Officer

You will work closely with Account, Creative, Project Management, and Business Affairs departments

Responsibilities

  • Assist in executing the creative concept and develop comprehensive production rationale with a innovation mindset
  • Identify and nurture emerging talent across various disciplines, fostering positive relationships with production partners
  • Source, evaluate, and negotiate production estimates, while coordinating and managing production schedules
  • Facilitate pre-production meetings, oversee production budgets, and ensure efficient project wrap-up and asset management
  • Provide expert guidance to agency stakeholders on content production matters, enhancing quality through creative and budgetary solutions
  • Demonstrate expertise in filmmaking, photography, digital platforms, and formats, while effectively juggling multiple projects
  • Mentor and support associate producers, and represent the agency in client-facing situations
  • Exhibit strong problem-solving skills with a proactive approach to navigating production challenges
  • Proficient in conflict resolution, upholding fiscal responsibility, and ethical standards
  • Comprehensive advertising acumen, including an understanding of strategies, platforms, and creative concepts
  • Ability to bring innovative ideas to the table, with a keen eye for visual aesthetics and understanding of music's role in content enhancement
  • Skilled in navigating contracts and financial/legal aspects associated with production, adaptable to the dynamic nature of production processes, and collaborative within a team environment
  • Experience in experiential production would be a bonus

Requirements

  • 7-10 years ad agency experience. Production experience a plus.
  • Knowledge of all agency disciplines
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Strong interpersonal and proactive problem-solving skills
  • Detail-oriented, take-ownership attitude, self-organizing and highly skilled in time management to facilitate involvement in multiple projects
  • Able to work independently with limited supervision, and communicate up, down and laterally to project stakeholders within the agency
  • Proficient in MAC OS, Excel, Word, PowerPoint, Keynote (Adobe Suite & Smartsheet a plus
