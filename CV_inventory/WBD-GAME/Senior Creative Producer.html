Your New Role

WB Games is looking for a Senior Creative Producer to lead a multi-track creative work across brand, UA/ASO, social, trailers, and live ops. This role turns business goals into clear plans, aligns teams and budgets, manages risk, and delivers high-quality assets on time and to spec. 

Your Role Responsibilities

  • Program ownership: Drive work from brief to final delivery across multiple workstreams and channels, coordinating internal teams (Creative, UA, Brand, Product, Legal/Loc) and external agencies. 

  • Planning & resourcing: Build schedules, staff internal teams, scope and onboard agencies, manage SOWs, and track budgets across both internal and partner work. 

  • Standards & quality: Hold internal and agency outputs to brand, legal/IP, localization, and technical specs; enforce review notes to final. 

  • Reviews & handoffs: Run structured checkpoints with in-house teams and vendors; keep intake, approvals, and deliveries clean and documented for all parties. 

  • Risk management: Surface issues early across internal and agency tracks, make calls, escalate when needed, and keep leaders informed with clear status. 

  • Launch readiness: Confirm trafficking, versioning, and metadata across internal pipelines and agency deliverables for global releases and live ops. 

  • Team leadership: Coach producers/coordinators and set expectations with agency leads; model calm, direct communication under deadline pressure. 

Qualifications & Experience

  • Deep experience with creative production (concept > edit > QA > ship) for video, motion, and static. 

  • Strong planning chops: timelines, dependencies, change control across parallel tracks. 

  • Clear, direct communication with creatives, marketers, product, and execs. 

  • Comfortable with project tools and asset systems (Asana/Jira, DAM). 

Great Fit Traits 

  • Systems thinker who sees both the board and the next move. 

  • Calm under pressure; decisive with incomplete information. 

  • Collaborative, candid, and solutions first. 

  • Led UA/ASO testing at scale and used results to guide the next cut. 

  • Global launch work (localization, cultural checks, legal/IP).
