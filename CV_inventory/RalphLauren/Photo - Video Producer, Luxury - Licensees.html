Position Overview

The Photo & Video Producer is involved all facets of producing advertising/digital/social photo and video shoots for Ralph Lauren’s Luxury brands and licensing as well as seasonal Fashion Show content and events. The Producer will partner with Global leaders in Marketing, Operations, Design and Creative to execute on a wide range of assets. This position will be responsible for both administrative and production work. 

Essential Duties & Responsibilities 

  • Work directly with in-house creative/art directors, stylists, design, casting, and operations on the development of shoot campaigns.
  • Research and propose talent to fit creative concepts.
  • Negotiate rates and usage and book relevant crew.
  • Source and partner with local production companies.
  • Generate detailed shoot estimates.
  • Coordinate and secure all travel arrangements for crews.
  • Manage all teams on set in partnership with hired production company.
  • Reconcile all shoot related billing and expenses.
