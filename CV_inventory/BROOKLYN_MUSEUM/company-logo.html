Senior Producer
 
Museum overview
The Brooklyn Museum is a world-renowned cultural institution dedicated to preserving and showcasing art, history, and heritage. Our mission is to bring people together through art and experiences that inspire celebration, compassion, courage, and the will to act. Our vision is to serve as an institution where art is a powerful force for personal transformation and social change. Celebrating our differences and learning about our diverse communities through our collections, exhibitions, and programming is the core of who we are and what we do. 

We are committed to creating and cultivating a community of talented, passionate people who are inspired to make the Museum an important hub of community activity and a great global destination. Our staff have the opportunity to grow their professional skills and work collaboratively with colleagues they admire and respect.


About the role
The Senior Producer (SP) will lead the production of content for the Brooklyn Museum’s Bloomberg Connects app. Collaborating with staff across the entire Museum, the SP will work with cross-departmental stakeholders, artists, thought-leaders in the community, and external voices to tell stories about the Museum’s special exhibitions and permanent collection. As lead content strategist and producer for the Bloomberg Connects app, the SP will oversee a multimedia content program (with a particular emphasis on audio), including researching and writing thematic proposals and scripts, talent coordination and vendor relations, project management, audio and video production (including pre- and post-production phases), and publication. The SP will deploy their finely tuned storytelling skills to make Brooklyn Museum content fun, informative, and accessible to art lovers around the world.
 
This is a grant-funded role available through December 31, 2027
 
Responsibilities:
  • Lead the content planning, production, publication, and evaluation for the Museum’s presence on the Bloomberg Connects app
  • Work with Marketing & Communications team members, partners across the Museum, and outside contributors and vendors to develop and execute audio guides and other digital content designed to enrich visitors’ experiences
  • Collaborate closely with the Senior Audio & Video Producer to ensure all content is in line with institutional content strategies and branding, and cross-distributed across platforms when possible
  • Develop content strategy for Bloomberg Connects, including identifying and prioritizing objects in exhibits to highlight; determining best content formats (text, audio, visual, other) to tell the story of works of art; and tailoring content to meet target audience needs and motivations
  • Lead all phases of content production, including pre-production for all audio content to appear in the Bloomberg app, e.g. brainstorming, research, and scripting; production, including audio recording or working in studio; and post-production, including editing and sound design
  • Identify opportunities to adapt Bloomberg Connects content for messaging campaigns, wider cultural events, breaking news, and/or memes across other platforms owned by the Museum, including social media, website, and email
  • Contribute to ongoing efforts aimed at digital accessibility for Bloomberg Connects.
  • Strategize the use of archival content, including from ASK, Google Arts & Culture, and other sources for Bloomberg Connects.
  • Oversee evaluations and data-driven reporting, including metrics for success based off of goal and KPI definition, usage tracking, and overall performance of digital guides
  • Ensure all audio content and collateral materials are logged, maintained, and archived in a standardized process and support logistics are executed in a timely manner
  • Serve as primary point of contact with Bloomberg Connects, including creating grant reporting documents in closes collaboration with the Development team
Requirements:
  • 5—7 years of digital content production experience at a media outlet and/or the digital wing of a mission-driven organization
  • Demonstrated experience in audio production, including scriptwriting, recording, editing and sound design
  • Excellent ability to communicate complex ideas clearly and effectively
  • Exceptional attention to consistency and detail
  • Exceptional organizational skills, proven ability to plan ahead and meet deadlines
  • Capacity to manage different workstreams and projects simultaneously, balancing short- and long-term deadlines
  • Experience with content management systems
  • Experience with managing and mentoring a junior colleague is preferred
  • Strong research, writing, and communication skills
  • Strong computer and mobile device literacy,
  • Commitment to working as part of a collaborative team
  • Ability to work effectively under pressure and adapt to shifting priorities
