### About Faire

Faire is an online wholesale marketplace built on the belief that the future is local — independent retailers around the globe are doing more revenue than Walmart and Amazon combined, but individually, they are small compared to these massive entities. At Faire, we're using the power of tech, data, and machine learning to connect this thriving community of entrepreneurs across the globe.

### About this role

The Lead Creative Producer will own and scale Faire's end-to-end creative production for global marketing campaigns. This role blends hands-on execution with strategic planning to ensure campaigns are delivered with clarity, speed, and creative excellence from concept through launch.

You will define and elevate how campaigns are produced—establishing scalable processes, improving cross-functional alignment, and driving operational rigor across Creative, Marketing, Growth, Product, and external partners.

### What you'll do

- Deliver high-impact, integrated global campaigns from concept through launch—on time, on budget, and at a high creative bar
- Create clear production strategies that align scope, timelines, and resourcing—reducing friction and accelerating execution
- Drive strong cross-functional alignment across Creative, Marketing, Growth, Product, and agency partners
- Build review and approval frameworks that improve clarity, speed, and creative quality
- Make thoughtful trade-offs that protect campaign impact while navigating ambiguity
- Steward production budgets and vendor partnerships to maximize value and efficiency
- Design and evolve scalable workflows that increase velocity without compromising craft
- Identify and eliminate systemic inefficiencies, leaving the organization stronger after every campaign
- Ensure every asset meets a high standard for creative excellence, technical precision, and launch readiness
- Provide leadership with clear visibility into progress, risks, and recommendations

### Qualifications

- 6–8+ years of experience in creative production, campaign operations, or agency leadership
- Proven track record leading large-scale, multi-channel campaigns across digital, video, social, paid, and owned channels
- Deep fluency in end-to-end creative workflows and production ecosystems
- Experience operating at a leadership level as a highly accountable individual contributor
- Demonstrated ability to balance strategic thinking with hands-on execution
- Strong operational rigor, creative judgment, and taste
- Experience developing, optimizing, and scaling systems, tools, and workflows
- Track record of driving operational change and aligning cross-functional teams
- Highly organized and structured, with the ability to apply process pragmatically
- Exceptional cross-functional influence skills, with the ability to drive alignment without direct authority
- Calm, decisive presence in high-pressure, high-visibility environments
