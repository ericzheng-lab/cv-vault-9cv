# Semafor - Executive Producer, Video & Audio

## Company Overview
Semafor is a global news platform that delivers news with context and clarity. The company focuses on creating high-quality video and audio content including flagship video podcasts.

## Position
**Executive Producer, Video & Audio**
- Location: New York, NY
- Type: Full-time, Executive
- Salary Range: $150,000 - $175,000

## Job Summary
Semafor is seeking an Executive Producer to lead and scale our fast-growing portfolio of video and audio shows, including flagship video podcasts. This is a senior, high-impact role responsible for end-to-end ownership of the portfolio - from development to production, distribution and commercial success.

## Key Responsibilities

### Portfolio Leadership
- Lead and scale Semafor's fast-growing portfolio of video and audio shows
- Oversee flagship video podcasts and audio content
- End-to-end ownership from development through production, distribution, and commercial success

### Production Management
- Set and own production schedules
- Make budget and resourcing trade-offs to ensure milestones are met
- Track and evaluate milestones for each production
- Ensure creative and technical vision guides all major decisions

### Content Development
- Participate in pitch meetings
- Evaluate potential projects for collaboration
- Read scripts and analyze production plans
- Develop new show concepts and formats

### Strategic Partnership
- Serve as strategic partner to senior stakeholders
- Drive transparent decision-making
- Ensure cross-organizational decisions align with Semafor objectives
- Address ambiguous problems including high-risk technical issues

### Team Leadership
- Provide direction and accomplish results through leading others
- Foster a collaborative, respectful environment
- Encourage crew to take risks for the good of projects
- Utilize existing systems or develop new production methods

## Requirements

### Minimum Qualifications
- Bachelor's degree or equivalent practical experience
- 8+ years of experience as a producer or production manager
- Experience in full production lifecycle - from pre-production through post production
- Experience working with filmmakers, musicians, creative directors, VFX artists, or animators
- Experience in digital media and content distribution

### Preferred Qualifications
- Experience in multiple entertainment verticals (e.g., Film and Advertising, Animation and Music, etc.)
- Experience producing within a news/media environment
- Experience translating between technical and creative partners
- Experience with podcast production and audio content

## Key Skills
- Video and audio production expertise
- Budget management and resource allocation
- Strategic planning and execution
- Cross-functional team leadership
- Content development and creative direction
- Commercial acumen for content monetization

## Company Culture
Semafor values:
- Global perspective with local relevance
- Context and clarity in storytelling
- Innovation in news delivery
- High-quality production standards
- Collaborative and respectful environment
