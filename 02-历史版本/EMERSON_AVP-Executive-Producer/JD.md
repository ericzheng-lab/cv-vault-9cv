# Emerson College - Assistant Vice President & Executive Producer, Cinematic Video Production

## Company Overview
Emerson College is a private college in Boston, Massachusetts, focused on communication and the arts. The college is known for its programs in film, television, journalism, marketing, and performing arts.

## Position
**Assistant Vice President & Executive Producer, Cinematic Video Production**
- Location: Greater Boston, MA
- Type: Full-time, Executive
- Salary Range: $131,900 - $168,200 annually
- Job Number: JR008611
- Grade: 23-14E

## Job Summary
The Assistant Vice President & Executive Producer of Cinematic Video Production leads the strategic vision, creative direction, and operational execution of high-impact video storytelling for the College's highest priority video production, under the leadership of the Marketing & Communications function. The AVP & Executive Producer oversees the development and production of cinematic-quality video content that advances institutional brand, enrollment, reputation, fundraising, and engagement goals. Serving as both a creative leader and executive-level strategist, the AVP & Executive Producer manages a portfolio of video initiatives from flagship brand films and campaign launches to digital content series and presidential communications.

## Key Responsibilities

### Strategic Leadership
- Develop and lead a comprehensive cinematic video strategy aligned with institutional marketing, enrollment, advancement, and brand objectives
- Serve as senior creative advisor to divisional and college leadership on visual storytelling and content direction
- Partner with senior administrators, academic leaders, and the college's enrollment and institutional advancement teams to identify high-impact storytelling opportunities
- Translate institutional priorities into compelling visual narratives that resonate with prospective students, families, alumni, donors, and key stakeholders

### Creative Direction & Production Oversight
- Oversee all phases of video production: concept development, scripting, budgeting, scheduling, filming, post-production, and distribution
- Set and maintain cinematic standards for visual identity, tone, and storytelling across all video content
- Approve creative briefs, scripts, treatments, and final edits to ensure alignment with brand and strategic goals
- Direct or supervise large-scale productions, including campaign films, broadcast-quality marketing, and documentary-style features
- Ensure accessibility compliance (captioning, audio description where appropriate) and platform optimization
- Provide creative oversight and input to all campus promotional video production, including production units and areas not under their direct oversight

### Team Leadership & Talent Management
- Lead and mentor an in-house video production team, including producers, directors, cinematographers, editors, and students
- Foster a collaborative, innovative and creative production culture

### Project & Budget Management
- Develop and manage annual production budgets
- Prioritize and allocate resources based on institutional impact and return on investment
- Implement efficient production workflows and scalable systems
- Manage production timelines and ensure on-time, on-budget delivery of projects
- Oversee freelance crews, agencies, and production vendors; negotiate contracts and manage performance expectations

### Brand Stewardship & Innovation
- Champion cinematic storytelling that elevates the College's brand and reputation
- Stay current with industry trends, emerging technologies, and distribution platforms
- Introduce innovative approaches in narrative structure, visual techniques, and digital content formats
- Measure performance metrics and audience engagement to inform future strategy
