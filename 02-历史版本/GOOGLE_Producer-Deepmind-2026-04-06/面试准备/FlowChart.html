💡 Key Reminders

STAR Method: Situation, Task, Action, Result. Keep hard metrics and data points ready.

Googleyness: Show how you thrive in ambiguity, respect diverse opinions, and do the right thing.

📌

Leadership: Focus on examples where you drove change and influenced teams without formal authority.

Compensation: Deflect early salary expectations. Let them state their band first during negotiations.
