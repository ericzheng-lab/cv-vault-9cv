📝 Cover Letter 写作指南

  • 固定结构：Header → Subject → Salutation → Opening → Body ×2 → Bullet List → Closing → Sign-off
  • 可变内容：Subject 行、Opening 钩子、Body 段落、Bullet 选择（从经验库选 2-3 个）
  • 写作策略：JD 偏离小 → 直接改写简历 bullets；偏离中 → 从经验库选材；偏离大 → 深度重构
  • 页数：CL 保持单页，约 300-400 词

Eric Zheng

Greater New York Area | eric.zhengyue@gmail.com | US Permanent Resident
Subject: Application for [职位名称] - Eric Zheng
[Dear Hiring Committee, / Dear [具体人名], / To the Recruitment Team,]

[钩子开场：As the Producer behind the Sundance and Berlinale selected feature... / With 15+ years managing $6M+ portfolios for global brands like Riot Games... / Having built post-production infrastructures from the ground up...]

[连接句：I am writing to express my strong interest in the [职位] role at [公司], bringing a unique blend of [根据岗位调整：independent prestige film experience / high-volume agency operations / technical post-production expertise].]

[第一层经验：电影制作 / 商业运营 / 创意领导 — 根据岗位侧重选择]

[第二层经验：补充另一个维度，展示广度]

[过渡句：Leveraging my [Executive MBA / 15-year track record / dual expertise], I approach this role as a strategic operation:]

  • [从经验库选第1个]: [用 STAR 结构展开：Situation → Action → Result]
  • [从经验库选第2个]: [量化结果，突出与岗位的相关性]
  • [从经验库选第3个，可选]: [技术工具 / 流程优化 / 团队建设]

[文化契合：Beyond technical execution, I share [公司]'s commitment to [diverse storytelling / innovation / excellence]. As a [Gold House member / EMBA graduate / Sundance alum], I am dedicated to [building culturally conscious pipelines / driving operational excellence / elevating brand narratives].]

[结尾：I look forward to discussing how my [具体能力] can contribute to [公司]. Thank you for your time and consideration.]

Sincerely,
Eric Zheng
